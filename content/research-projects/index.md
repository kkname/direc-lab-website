        Dr. Jeong's research team has secured over **$13.0 million** in funding from diverse sources including NSF, NCHRP, the Construction Industry Institute, FHWA, FTA, EPA, DOL, DOE, state DOTs, and industry sponsors.

        ## Active/Recent Projects (2023-2027)

        **Transformation of Undergraduate Education in Construction Science** (Oct 2024-Sep 2027)

        NSF-funded initiative creating serious games and simulations to teach complex project management concepts with improved student learning outcomes.

        ---

        **Aggie Construction Management Apprenticeship Program** (Oct 2024-Sep 2029)

        DOL-sponsored effort addressing skilled workforce shortages by creating worker-centered pathways in renewable energy, transportation, and broadband sectors across three locations.

        ---

        **Digital Twin-Based Semantic Interoperability** (Apr 2024-Dec 2026)

        International collaboration with Yonsei University developing AI-based semantic interoperability technologies for seamless asset data exchange in process plants.

        ---

        **State-wide Production Rate Estimation Tool** (Sep 2023-Feb 2026)

        TxDOT project leveraging historical data to create interactive heat maps visualizing feasible production rates for highway work items.

        ---

        **NSF REU Site: Smart and Sustainable Construction** (Sep 2023-Aug 2026)

        Undergraduate research experiences focusing on emerging digital technologies including VR, 3D-printing, drones, digital twins, and AI in construction.

        ---

        **AI Tool for Identifying Change Order Items** (Apr 2023-Aug 2026)

        TxDOT project using Natural Language Processing to systematically identify potentially missing work items in highway contracts.

        ## Completed Projects

        Notable completed research includes studies on pavement preservation effectiveness, contingency factors in cost estimation, integrated project delivery assessment, and work zone safety training development. Projects addressed diverse infrastructure challenges from bridge design cost estimation to granular road asset management systems.
