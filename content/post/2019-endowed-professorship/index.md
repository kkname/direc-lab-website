---
title: Dr. Jeong Appointed to James C. Smith CIAC Endowed Professorship
date: 2019-09-15
---

Dr. H. David Jeong has been appointed to hold the James C. Smith Construction Industry Advisory Council (CIAC) Endowed Professorship at Texas A&M University's College of Architecture.

<!--more-->

This prestigious endowed professorship supports Dr. Jeong's teaching, research, and service initiatives in construction science. The appointment recognizes his significant contributions to the field and his commitment to advancing construction education and research.

The James C. Smith CIAC Endowed Professorship was established to support faculty excellence in the Department of Construction Science and strengthen connections between academia and the construction industry.

Dr. Jeong will use this support to expand the DiReC Lab's research in data-driven construction management and AI applications, while also enhancing educational programs that prepare the next generation of construction professionals.
