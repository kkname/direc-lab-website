---
title: DiReC Lab Wins 2024 ASCE Best Paper Award
date: 2024-03-15
---

Dr. Jeong's research team has been awarded the 2024 Best Paper of the Year Award from the ASCE Journal of Construction Engineering and Management for their paper "Sequential Machine Learning for Activity Sequence Prediction from Daily Work Report Data."

<!--more-->

The paper, co-authored with Dr. Hamed Alikhani, Dr. Chau Le, and Dr. Ivan Damnjanovic, presents an innovative machine learning approach for predicting construction activity sequences from daily work reports. This research demonstrates the power of AI in improving construction project planning and scheduling.

This is the second time Dr. Jeong has received this prestigious award, having previously won in 2016 for research on international construction markets.

**Citation:**
Alikhani, H., Le, C., Jeong, H.D., and Damnjanovic, I. (2023) "Sequential Machine Learning for Activity Sequence Prediction from Daily Work Report Data" *Journal of Construction Engineering and Management, ASCE* 149(9). https://doi.org/10.1061/JCEMD4.COENG-1316
