---
title: Graduate Scholars Receive COSC Scholarships
date: 2023-04-15
---

Congratulations to DiReC Lab graduate scholars Suryeon Kim and John Oh for receiving scholarships from the Department of Construction Science at the 2023 COSC Scholarship Awards Ceremony!

<!--more-->

The annual scholarship awards ceremony recognizes outstanding graduate students in the Department of Construction Science for their academic excellence and research contributions.

Both Suryeon and John have made significant contributions to the lab's research in construction management and project delivery methods.
