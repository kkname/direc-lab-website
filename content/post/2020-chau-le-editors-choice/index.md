---
title: Chau Le's Research Selected as ASCE Editor's Choice Paper
date: 2020-05-20
---

Ph.D. student Chau Le's paper "Evaluating Contractors' Production Performance in Highway Projects Using Historical Daily Work Report Data" has been selected as an Editor's Choice paper by the ASCE Journal of Management in Engineering.

<!--more-->

The paper was featured in ASCE's Engineer's Toolbox, highlighting its practical applications for construction practitioners. This recognition showcases the impact of DiReC Lab's research in developing data-driven approaches for evaluating contractor performance.

Chau's research demonstrates how historical daily work report data can be leveraged to assess and improve contractor production performance in highway construction projects, providing valuable insights for project managers and decision-makers.
