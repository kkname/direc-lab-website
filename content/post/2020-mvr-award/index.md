---
title: Dr. Jeong Receives 2019 Most Valuable Researcher Award
date: 2020-04-15
---

Dr. H. David Jeong has been honored with the Department of Construction Science's 2019 Most Valuable Researcher Award, recognizing his outstanding research contributions to the field of construction science.

<!--more-->

The annual award celebrates faculty members who have made exceptional contributions to research in construction science through publications, grants, and innovative methodologies.

Dr. Jeong's research focuses on applying data analytics and artificial intelligence to construction management, with applications in project scheduling, cost estimation, and risk management. His work bridges academic research with practical industry needs, creating tangible impact in the construction sector.
